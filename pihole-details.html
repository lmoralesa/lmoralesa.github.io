Pi-hole

What is Pi-hole?

Pi-hole is a network-wide ad and tracker blocker. It works by blocking requests to known advertising and tracking domains, improving privacy and network performance.

Key Features and Uses

  • Blocks ads and trackers on all devices on your network.
  • Improves network speed and performance by reducing unnecessary traffic.
  • Enhances privacy by preventing tracking requests.
  • Easy to set up and manage (typically on a Raspberry Pi but can be on other systems).

Installation and Setup

Detailed installation instructions can be found on the official Pi-hole website. It involves installing on a server (often a Raspberry Pi) and configuring your network's DNS settings to point to the Pi-hole server.
