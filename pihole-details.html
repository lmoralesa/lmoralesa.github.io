Pi-hole

What is Pi-hole?

Pi-hole is a self-hosted, open-source network-wide ad and tracker blocker. It works by blocking DNS queries to known advertising and tracking domains, thereby improving privacy and network performance for all devices on your network.

Key Features and Benefits

  • Blocks ads and trackers across all devices connected to your network.
  • Significantly improves network speed and performance by reducing unnecessary DNS queries.
  • Enhances your privacy by preventing unwanted tracking requests.
  • Provides detailed statistics and analytics of blocked ads and queries via a web interface.
  • Relatively easy to set up and manage, often deployed on a Raspberry Pi but can run on other systems.

Installation and Setup

The installation process involves setting up a server (commonly a Raspberry Pi) and configuring your network's DNS settings to point to the Pi-hole server. Detailed, step-by-step instructions are available on the official Pi-hole website.
